The Future of AI

This is my favorite tech topic to talk about because it really allows me to express my thoughts on the subject. AI has gone so much farther than anyone thought it would in the past years. Going from people thinking that science is witchcraft back in the days to a time where Artificial Intelligence experts are in demand all over society in businesses and companies alike. This is an area that seems like it would be impossible to completetly figure out. Let's think about the human brain. Mankind has been in existence for centuries, and with all the scientific discoveries especially in the biological sector, the human brain hasn't been completely figured out. The fact that the world of AI is based on brain activity and how it works makes it all the more absurd to think we can fully replicate that. But we're getting somewhere because "the journey of a thousand miles begins with a step". If you've ever seen Alita: Battle Angel, which is still one of my favorite movies, you immediately realize that androids are not too far off from where we are at the moment. Only thing is that getting there is going to take a lot more work than we think. From your typical website chatbots to virtual assistants like that of the Google Home or Amazon's Alexa, it's evident that we edge closer to that point everyday. This is made possible through the way data is manipulated and processed to give feedback.
As long as we find more efficient ways to collect and use data, we are heading towards a place of insurmountable possibilities. I aspire to be someone that contributes substantially to this future as I continue to hone and develop my skills because I want to see how far we can push ourselves beyond our limits.
